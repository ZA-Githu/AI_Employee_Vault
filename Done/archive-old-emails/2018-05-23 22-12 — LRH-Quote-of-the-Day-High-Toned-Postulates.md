Daily LRH Quote

“You get him up to a point where he can undo or redo his postulates on his own decision. ...

“He now has the power to postulate. And woe betide the enemies this person has, because if—if this postulatingness continued on downscale below the level where it actually lives, it would just be the saddest, sorriest world you ever heard of if people at 1.5 could postulate and make it stick. And they can’t. Or if people at Boredom could make their postulates stick; they can’t.

“They can make their postulates stick only above the point where they always keep a bargain. Isn’t that an interesting commentary on Man?”

— L. Ron Hubbard
