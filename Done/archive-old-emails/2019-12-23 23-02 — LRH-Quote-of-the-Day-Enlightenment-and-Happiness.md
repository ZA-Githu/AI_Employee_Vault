LRH Quote of the Day

“In the lands of the greatest mysteries, I found the greatest unhappiness. In the areas of the world where all was a mystery and nobody was in communication at all, everybody was starving to death and falling on their heads.

“So we have to assume that in those areas where there is the greatest enlightenment, we would have the greatest happiness. And it's true.

“If Man wishes to be happy, if Man wants to be happy, then Man will have to be able to know.”

— L. Ron Hubbard
