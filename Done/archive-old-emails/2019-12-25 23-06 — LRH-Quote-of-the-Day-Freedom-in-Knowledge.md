LRH Quote of the Day

“The greater beingness there is, the greater power, the greater happiness on the part of the individual, the greater knowledge of an individual's single capabilities and the less significance behind things. ...

“Do you know that case after case has broken simply because the auditor made the individual do something without any reason? ...

“You could say the greatest happiness there is would be to discover there wasn't any significance to anything. That would give you complete liberty to do as you pleased. And that in itself is a freedom in knowledge.”

— L. Ron Hubbard
