LRH Quote of the Day

“The area you can determine or influence is directly regulated by the amount of communication you have into that area no matter how painful it may be to you. Remember that.

“The determinism of the actions in that area directly and immediately depends upon the amount of communication you’ve got into that area.

“And the answer is more communication, not less.”

— L. Ron Hubbard
