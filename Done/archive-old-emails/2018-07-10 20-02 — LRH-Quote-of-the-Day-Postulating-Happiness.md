LRH Quote of the Day

“What would happen if a fellow went down the street saying, ‘I'm happy. I'm happy. I'm happy. I'm happy.’ Well, something would happen very peculiar if he was using the words, the symbols, ‘I am happy’—probably repeat himself back down the time track into birth or something. ‘I am so happy it's a boy,’ his mother was saying. [laughter] But if he actually got the idea—nonverbal—the idea he's happy, the idea he was successful, the idea he was Cleared, he'd probably bang three feet back of his head so fast it would make him quite dizzy.”

— L. Ron Hubbard
